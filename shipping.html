Domestic Shipping Policy

If we are experiencing a high volume of orders, shipments may be delayed by a few days. Please allow additional days in transit for delivery. If there will be a significant delay in the shipment of your order, we will contact you via email or telephone.   Shipping rates and delivery estimates Shipping charges for your order will be calculated and displayed at checkout.
